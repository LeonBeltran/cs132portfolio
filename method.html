$$\text{aggression score} = S + C_i$$

\(\text{Where: }\)

\(S := \text{Number of swear words in tweet}\)

\(C_i := \text{Capital Word Index}\)


\(\text{and let } x\text{ be the percentage of all-capital words in a tweet} \)

\(C_i = \begin{cases} 0,& x < 0.1 \\ 1,& 0.1 \leq x < 0.3\\ 2,& 0.3 \leq x < 0.6 \\ 3,& x \geq 0.6 \end{cases}\)
