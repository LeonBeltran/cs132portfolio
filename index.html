CS132: Group 33

Stigma Against LGBTQ and STDs

Problem

Research question: Have sexually transmitted diseases (STDs) such as HIV or AIDS, or diseases commonly attributed to STDs like monkeypox, caused an increased negative stigma on the LGBTQ+ community?

Hypothesis: The diseases have caused an increase in the negative stigma against the LGBTQ+ community.

Null Hypothesis: The diseases have not caused an increase in the negative stigma against the LGBTQ+ community.

Action Plan: Analyze the use and tone of language in tweets spreading mis/disinformation about the LGBTQ+ community regarding STDs or diseases attributed to STDs.
